school

I'm a second year, computer science student at the University of Wyoming. I spend most of my time in the computer science department learning about cybersecurity, and the rest of my academic time pursuing graphic design and creative writing. My academic interests include things such as industrial control systems security, cybersecurity education, and interactive systems design. I also volunteer in the Cybersecurity Education and Research lab on campus, CEDAR.

code

I spend most of my time working in Python and JavaScript, but I also do work in Java, C#, C++, and Rust. Professionally I am developing software to teach cybersecurity and sys-admin principles, but for personal work, I like to make IoT devices that serve little function, design games, and put together my own systems to streamline the student experience. Check out my work over on Github.

writing

I'm an amateur author currently writing my first novel. I write predominantly in the science-fiction and fantasy genres with a special passion for cyberpunk worlds. As work progresses to a point worth talking about, the projects page of this website will be updated to reflect the ongoing writing process. Currently I am studying creative writing at the University of Wyoming.

youtube

I've done a variety of projects on YouTube including running multiple channels. They've spanned gaming let's play channels, news work, entertaining daily show style content, daily vlog content, video essays, and, most recently, detailing the process of building a startup from beginning to end on video.

The most noteworthy of these adventures was my work at OneTV is the most noteworthy of these. This was a studio at the University of Wyoming where I produced the show 'source' that was a mix between news, information, and student life.

I currently reside over at Andey Robins where I'm documenting my work to create a cybersecurity education startup.
